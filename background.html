A Brief Review of Literature

In the study of LGBTQ+ rights and homelessness, several facets have already been covered. It is also known that 5.6% of adults identify as LGBTQ+, and 1 in 6 adults born since 1997 identify as LGBTQ+. (Jones 2021). This literature, however, does leave several important questions unanswered. While homelessness in the transgender community has been examined, homelessness in the LGBTQ+ community at large has not been examined. As such, this study will explore the homelessness among the LGBTQ community under the age of 25.
